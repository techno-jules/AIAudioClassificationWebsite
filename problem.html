CHALLENGES

According to researchers from JAMA Otolaryngology, hearing impaired members are 90% more likely to be injured outside their homes.

High-quality hearing aids are expensive as most cost thousands of dollars.

Besides, hearing aids are specifically made to identify human speech and not non-speech audio.

There are not many robust technical solutions that detect non-word sounds and ML technology is still new and not widely in use.

There are not many accessible/free audio datasets to train ML solutions with.

Many existing audio ML algorithms are inaccurate or too large to deploy as a real-world solution.

PERSONAL CONNECTION

My grandma is hearing impaired.

  • The hearing aids she could buy with the money she has are not very effective, as family members still need to raise our voices to talk to her and supervise her outside. However, there are times when no family member is available to take her outside.

There needs to be an accessible, affordable, AND accurate solution.

  • We need to build a more optimized solution for hearing impaired members, especially in low-income communities.
